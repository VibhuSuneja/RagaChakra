# RagaChakra - 3-Minute Pitch Script

## 0:00 – 0:20 | The Hook (The Problem)
**Action:** *Stand on stage. Screen shows the empty desktop (or just a title slide).*
**Script:**
"Indian Classical Music is a 3,000-year-old science of human emotion. Certain scales—called Ragas—are strictly meant to be heard only at specific times of day to heal specific emotional states. 
But if you're a new listener opening Spotify, you have no idea where to start. Recommendation algorithms suggest music based on what's popular. They don't know that you're overwhelmed, and they don't know the sun is setting. 
Enter **RagaChakra**—your AI ritual companion."

## 0:20 – 0:45 | Onboarding & Hybrid Architecture
**Action:** *Press `Ctrl + Shift + D` (Judge Mode) OR open the app and quickly tap through onboarding.*
**Script:** 
"RagaChakra isn't just a playlist generator; it's a guide. 
Under the hood, we use a Hybrid AI Pipeline. First, a deterministic Rule Engine calculates the exact solar time based on our geolocation, identifying the current 'Prahar'. It filters our database for ragas strictly permitted for this window—ensuring traditional accuracy.
Then, we pass that shortlist to Gemini 2.5 Flash, along with our psychological profile and current mood—in this case, 'Overwhelmed'. Gemini selects the perfect raga and designs a personalized ritual."

## 0:45 – 1:15 | AI Thinking & Explainability
**Action:** *Show the AI Thinking loader, then the Recommendation Screen appears.*
**Script:** 
"Notice the transparency. The AI explains its reasoning. We aren't just handed a song; we're told *why*. 
It recommends Yaman. It's the evening, which is Yaman's natural home. Our mood calls for stillness. And we have a 92% confidence score based on the intersection of circadian rhythm and our emotional state."

## 1:15 – 2:00 | The Audio & The Ritual
**Action:** *Click play on the YouTube embed for 3 seconds so the judges hear the music. Then click "Begin Ritual".*
**Script:** 
"We can preview the music immediately. But RagaChakra is about intentional listening. 
When we begin the ritual, we are guided through a three-stage timer. Close your eyes, breathe, and listen to the Alap without distractions."
**Action:** *Click the 'Skip (Demo)' button to bypass the 15-minute timer and land on the Reflection screen.*

## 2:00 – 2:40 | Reflection & AI Memory
**Action:** *Type "It made the room feel a lot larger and my thoughts quieter." into the reflection journal and click Complete.*
**Script:** 
"After listening, the ritual asks for a single reflection. When we submit this, Gemini processes our journal entry and synthesizes it into a core emotional memory. 
When we return to the dashboard tomorrow..."
**Action:** *Point to the Memory Card at the top of the dashboard.*
"...RagaChakra remembers. We don't just have an app; we have an evolving, intelligent companion that learns how different ragas affect our specific emotional topology."

## 2:40 – 3:00 | The Close
**Action:** *Leave the screen on the beautiful Dashboard with the Memory Card.*
**Script:** 
"RagaChakra bridges ancient acoustic science with cutting-edge LLMs. By combining a rigid circadian Rule Engine with the empathetic reasoning of Gemini, we've created a deeply personal, explainable, and beautiful music experience.
